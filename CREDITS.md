# Credits

Perfect Dark Renaissance exists because of years of work by the Perfect Dark, GoldenEye, decompilation, modding, and PC-port communities.

## Perfect Dark Renaissance

- Development and release preparation: Beyond Synth
- Additional maps and community work: Oodark, Oofjay, MrKane, Sogun, Garabuyo, Trevor, Comet, TimeH, and EternallyAries

## Foundations

- Perfect Dark and GoldenEye 007 were originally developed by Rare and published by Nintendo.
- Ryan Dwyer and contributors: [Perfect Dark decompilation project](https://github.com/n64decomp/perfect_dark), reverse engineering, documentation, and `pd-extract`.
- fgsfdsfgs and contributors: [Perfect Dark PC port](https://github.com/fgsfdsfgs/perfect_dark).
- Jonaeru and Atari-Dude: Perfect Dark All-in-One Mod work on which this project was built.
- Wreck and contributors: GoldenEye X.
- Raf: `pd_blendtools` and related tooling.

## Upstream port acknowledgements

- doomhack and contributors for an earlier public Perfect Dark porting effort.
- The sm64-port contributors for audio-mixer and related work used by the upstream port.
- The Ship of Harkinian/libultraship, Emill, and MaikelChan contributors for Fast3D work used by the upstream port.
- lieff for minimp3.
- Mouse Injector and 1964GEPD contributors for mouselook and high-frame-rate research and fixes.
- NicNamSam for upstream icon work.
- Everyone who submitted fixes, testing, research, maps, tools, documentation, or issue reports to the projects above.

This list is intended as a good-faith acknowledgement and may be expanded when additional contributors are identified.
